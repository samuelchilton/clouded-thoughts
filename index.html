Welcome

Welcome to Clouded Thoughts, a place where ideas float freely like clouds in the sky. Clouds you leave behind are shared with other users until they are supplanted by the next 100 ideas.
This app was created by me, Sam, as a way to experiment with Claude Code. It's pretty amazing how easy it was to create this.
This app collects no user data. It accepts thoughts that you submit, generates a timestamp in UTC time, and collects nothing else. It is also unmoderated, so please do not post illegal or rude things here.
